When you export a new animation, you need to add these to the sauerbraten iqm exporter!

IMPORTANT! Export scale needs to be 0.80!
IMPORTANT! these need to be in this order







head:
head-tilt, base-stand-to-crouch, head-tilt-crouch

torso:
arms-stand,arms-walk,arms-run,arms-aiming,arms-cycle-gun,arms-toggle-safety,arms-into-fighting,arms-punch,arms-craft,arms-eat,base-stand-to-crouch,arms-crouch,arms-crouch-walk,arms-crouch-aiming,arms-crouch-cycle-gun,arms-crouch-toggle-safety,arms-crouch-into-fighting,arms-crouch-punch,arms-crouch-craft,arms-crouch-eat



legs:
legs-stand,legs-walk,legs-run,base-stand-to-crouch,legs-crouch,legs-crouch-walk





















Note: When creating an item/weapon mod, attach item to palm bone
then export it with the torso animation, it will automatically attach

Note: Create a special arm bone position called "item" that has the item
on the ground at 0,0,0

Note: Clothing will render on top of player, attach clothing to player bones
